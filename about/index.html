Background

I have trained in science, technology, and business with an appreciation for the arts. My focus is to develop and enhance money making business objectives with the wise and timely use of electronics, software, programming, internet technologies, vehicles, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and more.

In science, I am trained in chemical and biological laboratory science, learning how our world works on a fundamental level, and worked with researchers on mobile genetics in disrupting pathogenic rainwater organisms, as well as designing and quantifying pilot-scale chemical reactors. I further specialised in extraction technology, such as extracting catnip oil from the catnip plant, to further use as a safe mosquito repellant.


In technology I use software to define everything, automation in Microsoft Power Automate, software-defined radio, Salesforce Tableau and Python for data analysis, and using computers for everything from study to creation. My current area of research is how to build an effective, robust, and scalable automated greenhouse using Arduinos, Raspberry Pis, and other consumer electronics and software to replace most of the current legacy, expensive hardware. Importantly, after becoming an official software developer in post-secondary education, I decided to return to college and learn how to effectively develop different types of AI systems.


None of this could be accomplished without grounding in reality, in business. I studied business development, supply chain management, international business, community economic development, sustainability, change management, marketing, and importantly leadership. I also love talking with entrepreneurs and investors!


My education and experience fit well together because I can worth technical and non-technical people, and in fact stakeholders of all types. This has brought me to many work settings including fixing printers and teaching computer usage at all levels, fixing domain controller issues, debugging E-mail routing and authenticity issues, data governance and management, regulatory compliance for data and websites, setting up servers for various purposes including web services, VPNs, and hypervisors. Then I'm off to a meeting to help an entrepreneur with a marketing campaign, and helping them build a more robust supply chain risk management plan.


In my free time, I am outdoors flying a drone, improving my archery skills, camping, or taking a drive to somewhere new! In the winter, I'm watching travel videos on YouTube, in the kitchen, researching something I can eventually publish, playing with tech, or developing the next big thing!
