Introduction: Gordon Stevens

Introduction

Gordon Stevens

Gordon Stevens is a Canadian based in Toronto, Canada and the Greater Toronto Area. With over a decade of work experience in business, software development, information technology, security, education, and postgraduate research Gordon has an excellent mix of experiences in several industries with a focus on building profitability and corporate sustainability through corporate social responsibility and effective leadership.

Education is an excellent way to build a foundation for experience, and Gordon actively pursues it. Previously, Gordon's education included education in Chemical Technology, Software Development, Business Administration, International Business, Import and Export Business, International Trade Specialization, and Supply Chain Management/Global Logistics.

Gordon is currently pursuing masters degrees in Business Leadership and Management, Business Administration and Leadership, as well as Education in Data Science, including big data, data management, artificial intelligence, and data analysis for business.

Gordon is very passionate about small and medium enterprise (SME) business as it composes over 99% of Canadian business, and entrepreneurship is the foundation of all business excellence. SMEs often lack access tools and knowledge which larger businesses use, such as data analytics to discover trends and emergent patterns, which help predict future demand for products and services. Corporate social responsibility, also known as the triple-bottom-line was created to seek enrichment to people, planet, and profitability from business activity. Focusing on people and the environment, in addition to profits helps many businesses create a much more enriched culture in the workplace, not to mention much more sustainable. Companies who practice this often have been afforded an elevated position in the marketplace to create a 100-year sustainability plan, such as Shopify.

In technology, Gordon has specialized in web development, creating secure corporate web portals, websites with full marketing campaigns including a Wordpress front website optimized for search engine discovery, as well as copywriting content, newsletter integration with services like Survey Monkey and Constant Contact, with a project and sales backend system.

In all industries which Gordon participates in, it is important to have education and experience in business. This is illustrated well in Software Development, which requires attention to stakeholder management, productivity, return on investment, advertising, customer engagement, and brand management, with other attention to business details.
