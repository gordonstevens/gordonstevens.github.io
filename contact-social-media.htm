On the Internet

I started with Facebook early on as a means of keeping a digital address book and as a way to keep in contact with people in my life. Eventually with the explosion of Facebook Games I used to play FarmVille. I got the chance to meet a lot of new people on Facebook this way. Eventually Facebook became both a place to learn about chocolate and bread making, and a blighted intellectual property at the same time. Having used WhatsApp in the past, I found that it leaked too much information and data, so I have a seperate phone and number for WhatsApp. I generally use other platforms. With this said, I still enjoy using Instagram to photograph and showcase the Greater Toronto Area.
