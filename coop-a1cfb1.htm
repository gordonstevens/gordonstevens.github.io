Research Assistant

Western University Research Assistant

At Western University, through a Co-Operative Education Program (Co-Op), I was able to assist Masters and Ph.D. Students with their research. Each university lab was supervised by a faculty member, and I worked day-to-day, side-by-side with graduate students working on their research for their Masters, Ph.D. and Post-doctoral fellow degrees.

In my Research Assistance position in Chemical Engineering in Prof. Jesse Zhu's lab, under the guidance of Ph.D. student Dr. Dongbing Li, we were quantifying all aspects of a circulating fluidized bed chemical reactor.
