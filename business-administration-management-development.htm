BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION AND DEVELOPMENT

At the intersection of Science, Art, and Business

From childhood education in business has been a core driver in my eductional journey. From learning about working in a unionised environment, to the subtle and not so subtle effects of politics, weather, supply and demand, and various market conditions upon business and opportunities, to helping, growing, and ultimately consulting within the family business. Now consulting for businesses within technical markets, with focus on marketing, strategic planning, and strategic leadership.

In my current capacity I am a Financial Markets Analyst, participating in Crypto Currency, as well as the stock market. Another market of interest includes foreign currency exchange markets ("Forex"). Gordon Stevens also consults for web development studios, photography studios (drones), education delivery businesses, start-ups, and food service sector businesses.

Finance

Where is the Money?

Sometimes having money becomes almost secondary to how the money is stored, especially in multinational corporations with satellite offices or even large operations in countries not under direct leadership of the home office. Currency fluctuations, shifts in government policy, supply chain blips or complete melt-downs, competition sneaking up in a market, how will your business react? Move assets? Exchange to crypto? These are all positons and more which must be considered.
